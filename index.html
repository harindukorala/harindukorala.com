Harindu Korala

Melbourne, Australia. hkorala@swin.edu.au

I am an AI and Data Science Research Fellow at the Monash Data Futures Institute, Monash University, where I work with a set of leading cross-disciplinary experts to enhance health sciences, governance and policy and sustainable development using data-driven AI. I completed my doctoral research at Swinburne University of Technology and I hold a B.Sc. Hons degree in Information Technology from University of Moratuwa (Sri Lanka). From 2015 to 2018, I worked as a Senior Software Engineer at 99X Technology (Sri Lanka).

My research interests are Internet of Things, Artificial Intelligence, Data analytics at cloud and edge, Cloud computing, Machine learning
